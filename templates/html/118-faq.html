Getting Started

Visit our sign-up page and fill in your details. You will receive a verification email to confirm your account.
Our platform works in all modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). No installation required.
After signing in, go to Settings > Profile to update your personal information, avatar, and preferences.

Billing & Plans

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans.
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. Contact support for assistance.

Features & Usage

The number of users depends on your plan: Starter (1), Professional (10), Enterprise (unlimited).
Yes, you can export all your data in CSV, JSON, or PDF format from Settings > Data Export.
Yes, our REST API is available on Professional and Enterprise plans with comprehensive documentation.

Security & Privacy

Yes, all data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We follow industry best practices.
Yes, we support TOTP-based two-factor authentication via apps like Google Authenticator or Authy.
Data is stored in SOC 2 Type II certified data centers in the US and EU. You can choose your region.
